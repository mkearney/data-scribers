First release of mlrMBO - the toolbox for (Bayesian) Black-Box Optimization

We are happy to finally announce the first release of mlrMBO on cran after a quite long development time. For the theoretical background and a nearly complete overview of mlrMBOs capabilities you can check our paper on mlrMBO that we presubmitted to arxiv. The key features of mlrMBO are: Global optimization of expensive Black-Box functions. Multi-Criteria Optimization. Parallelization through multi-point proposals.
